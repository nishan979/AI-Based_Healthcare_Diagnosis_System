Prof. Dr. Shishir Basak is a renowned cardiologist in Bangladesh with over 15 years of experience in advanced cardiac care. Dedicated to patient well-being, he combines international expertise with personalized treatment plans tailored to the needs of Bangladeshi patients

Professional statement

I strive to deliver world-class cardiac care accessible to all. My practice focuses on accurate diagnosis, compassionate communication, and sustainable heart health solutions for every patient.

Trained in the UK and Bangladesh, Dr. Basak specializes in both preventive cardiology and complex interventions, ensuring comprehensive care for conditions like hypertension, heart failure, and coronary artery disease.

Specializations
  • Interventional Cardiology
  • Preventive Cardiology
  • Heart Failure & Arrhythmia Treatment
  • Echocardiography & Non-Invasive Testing
  • Hypertension & Diabetes-Related Heart Care

Education

  • Royal College of Physicians, London - MRCP (UK)
  • Dhaka Medical College Hospital - Residency in Internal Medicine
